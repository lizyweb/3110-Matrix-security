About Us

Leading Security & Facility Service Provider

We introduce ourselves as one of the leading Security & Facility service provider managed by well experienced professionals. Ours is an ISO 9001, 14001 : 2015 certified company.

We are successfully running our organization with more than 250 iconic clients, and we have manpower value of more than 1000 in all over Tamilnadu.

We providing various type of manpower like Armed guards (Ex-service & Civilian), Unarmed Guards, Security Officers, House Keepers, Casual Labours and all type of Contract labours to Various type of sectors like Public Sectors, Private Organizations, Banks, Financial Institutions, Bank Stressed Properties, Retails Outlets, Automobile Showrooms, Colleges, Hospital, Manufacturing Units, Apartments and Toll gates.
